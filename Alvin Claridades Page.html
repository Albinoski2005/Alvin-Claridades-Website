Atty. Alvin Claridades

Lawyer | Engineer | Educator

About Me

Atty. Alvin Claridades is a multifaceted professional with a strong background in law, engineering, and education. He currently serves as the Corporate Secretary of the Laguna Lake Development Authority (LLDA) in the Philippines and has taught law at various universities, including Polytechnic University of the Philippines, New Era University, UDM, UA&P, and Wesleyan University of the Philippines. He is widely recognized for his expertise in environmental law, housing laws, election law, civil procedure, and constitutional law.
